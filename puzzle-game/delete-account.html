How to Delete Your Data

üì± Good to know: Number Grid Puzzle does not require an account. All your game progress, scores, settings, and purchase status are stored locally on your device only ‚Äî nothing is uploaded to our servers. Deleting app data or uninstalling the app removes everything completely and instantly.

What Data Exists?

When you play Number Grid Puzzle, the following is saved locally on your device:

  • Your best score
  • Your current level progress
  • Sound preference (on / off)
  • "Remove Ads" purchase status

None of this data is tied to an account or stored on any external server.

Option 1: Uninstall the App (Recommended)

Uninstalling the app permanently removes all local data.

Android

  1. Open Settings on your device.
  2. Tap Apps (or Application Manager).
  3. Find and tap Number Grid Puzzle.
  4. Tap Uninstall and confirm.

Alternative: Long-press the app icon on your home screen ‚Üí drag to Uninstall.

iOS (iPhone / iPad)

  1. Find Number Grid Puzzle on your home screen.
  2. Long-press the icon until the menu appears.
  3. Tap Remove App ‚Üí Delete App.
  4. Confirm by tapping Delete.

Option 2: Clear App Data (Keep App Installed)

This resets all progress and scores to zero while keeping the app installed.

Android

  1. Open Settings.
  2. Tap Apps ‚Üí find Number Grid Puzzle.
  3. Tap Storage & Cache.
  4. Tap Clear Storage (or Clear Data) and confirm.

iOS (iPhone / iPad)

  1. Go to Settings ‚Üí General ‚Üí iPhone Storage.
  2. Tap Number Grid Puzzle.
  3. Tap Delete App, then reinstall from the App Store.

Note: iOS does not provide a standalone "Clear Data" option ‚Äî deleting and reinstalling achieves the same result.

Option 3: In-App Reset (If Available)

If the app includes a reset feature:

  1. Open Number Grid Puzzle.
  2. Navigate to Settings.
  3. Look for Reset Progress or Clear All Data.
  4. Follow the on-screen confirmation prompts.
