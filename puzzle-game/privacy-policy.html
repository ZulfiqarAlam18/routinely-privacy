Privacy Policy for Number Grid Puzzle

Last Updated: March 1, 2026

Thank you for playing Number Grid Puzzle ("we", "our", or "us"). We respect your privacy and are committed to being transparent about how the app handles your information. This policy explains what data is collected, how it is used, and your rights regarding that data.

1. Information We Collect

Number Grid Puzzle is a free-to-play puzzle game supported by ads and optional in-app purchases. As a result, the following types of information may be collected:

1a. Data Stored Locally on Your Device

  • Game progress & scores: Your current level, best score, tile history, and in-app purchase status (e.g. whether ads have been removed) are saved locally on your device only.
  • App settings: Preferences such as sound on/off are stored locally.

This local data is never transmitted to our servers.

2. How We Use Your Data

  • Local game data is used solely to save and restore your progress.
  • Advertising data (collected by AdMob) is used to display relevant ads and measure ad performance.
  • Purchase data (processed by Google Play) is used only to unlock the ad-free experience.

We do not use any data for profiling, selling to third parties, or any purpose beyond operating the app.

3. Data We Do NOT Collect

  • We do not require account sign-up or collect your name, email address, or any personally identifiable information.
  • We do not track your location.

  • We do not sell your data to third parties.
