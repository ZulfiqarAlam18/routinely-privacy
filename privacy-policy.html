Privacy Policy for Routinely

Last Updated: January 13, 2026

Thank you for choosing Routinely ("we", "our", or "us"). We are committed to protecting your privacy and ensuring the security of your personal information. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and safeguard your data when you use our mobile application.

1. Information We Collect

Routinely may collect the following types of information:

  • Email Address: Used for account creation and authentication purposes.
  • Name: Used to personalize your experience within the app.
  • Habit Tracking Data: Information about your habits, goals, progress, and tracking history that you input into the app.

2. How Your Data is Stored

üîí Local Storage Only: All your personal data, including your email, name, and habit tracking information, is stored locally on your device. We do not store your data on external servers.

This means:

  • Your data remains on your device at all times
  • You have complete control over your information
  • Your habits and personal details are never transmitted over the internet
  • No cloud backup of personal data occurs

3. Data We Do NOT Collect

We want to be transparent about what we don't do:

  • We do not send your data to external servers
  • We do not sell your information to third parties
  • We do not share your data with advertisers
  • We do not track your location
  • We do not collect device identifiers for advertising purposes

4. Data Security

We take the security of your data seriously. Since all data is stored locally on your device:

  • Your data is protected by your device's built-in security features
  • We recommend using device-level security (PIN, fingerprint, face recognition)
  • The app does not require an internet connection to function

5. Third-Party Services

Routinely does not integrate with third-party analytics, advertising, or tracking services that would compromise your privacy. Any future integrations will be clearly disclosed in this policy.

6. Children's Privacy

Routinely is not intended for children under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. If you believe a child has provided us with personal information, please contact us immediately.

7. Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Access: View all your data within the app at any time
  • Delete: Remove all your data by uninstalling the app or clearing app data
  • Control: Manage what information you input into the app

8. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by posting the new Privacy Policy on this page and updating the "Last Updated" date. We encourage you to review this Privacy Policy periodically.

9. Data Deletion

For information on how to delete your account and all associated data, please visit our Delete Account/Data page.
